About the Book

K.V. Sridhar (Pops)
Founder & Chief Creative Officer,
HyperCollective

K.V.Sridhar aka Pops, is one of the most celebrated ad-makers of our country. From humble beginnings as a Bollywood film billboard painter to becoming the National Creative Director of Leo Burnett, to spearheading SapientNitro's creative operations in India, to embarking on an entrepreneurial journey with HyperCollective, Pops has seen it all.

In a career spanning over thirty-five years, he has helped build some of the leading brands like Coca Cola, Thums-Up, Limca, Mazza, Samsung, LG, GM, to name a few. He has won over 22 Cannes Lions and over 100 international awards.
And was recently named in Campaign Asia's prestigious Hall of Fame of Asian Advertising, that honoured media and marketing leaders of the past forty years.

The creator of the Gandhi Font, a brilliant artist who is famous by the name iPops, an ace cook who loves his food, a man of simplicity.
