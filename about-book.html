About the Book

KV Sridhar's romance with advertising and life is the genesis of this book, 30 Second Thrillers. A book that captures the magic which has unfolded on our TV screens over the last 30 years as 30 seconders.

It takes the readers to the world where the creative oompa-loompas of advertising, work tirelessly to create the 30 seconders that become a part of peoples lives. A book that tells the stories behind the 30 second story - how was it made, when did the idea spark like a bulb, who thought of it and when, the anecdotes, the efforts all of it captured in words! Further ornamented by the inputs and insights of the makers themselves.

A book that covers every great ad-maker and their story, starting from the vintage ads like
'Chal meri luna', 'Humara Bajaj' to the new 'Har ek friend zaroori hota hai', the book revisits all. From the days of Doordarshan to the YouTube of today, the journey is captured succulently in this book. It's an ode to our common viewers, our aunts and uncles, our neighbours, that stranger who we have had a random conversation with in our train journey.
